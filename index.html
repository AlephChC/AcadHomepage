    My research interest includes historical linguistics and Chinese linguistics, esp. phonology and phonetics. Furthermore, the methodologies used in Tibeto‑Burman comparison for reconstructing Old Chinese constitute another key area of focus for me. I also take an interest in topics such as language evolution, linguistic typology, and the philosophy of linguistics.

    I graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from the College of Information Engineering and Art Design at Zhejiang University of Water Resources and Electric Power (浙江水利水電學院). I have participated in two academic conferences on Wu languages, in 2018 and 2022. I have also conducted some personal fieldwork on language studies.
